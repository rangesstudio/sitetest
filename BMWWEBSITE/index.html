About Us

We are a small group of nerds originated in Algonquin, Illinois. For a fairly cheap price we will modify your vehicle to have helpful features that cost a lot when buying the car at a dealership or else where.

How It Works?

Step 1. You pick a package and send us an email with your address, BMW model, features that you would like as to add, and the time at which you are available

Step 2. We come to the location you have listed and connect our system to your car and begin our work.

Step 3. You check that everything works as you have requested and we take the payment.

Hours Of Work

We are ready to receive and respond to your email or call anytime, but the modification should either happen during the weekends or somewhere after 5 P.M on the week days.
